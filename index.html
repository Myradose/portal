Concept

Context Engineering

The paradigm shift from crafting individual prompts to designing the entire information environment an AI model operates in - tools, knowledge, and instructions working together.

Concept

Environment Engineering

The next evolution: isolated, observable, retrievable environments where AI agents can safely tackle complex development work without interfering with each other or your codebase.
